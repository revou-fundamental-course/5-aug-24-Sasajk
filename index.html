Kalkulator BMI

The Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator can be used to calculate BMI value and corresponding weight status while taking age into consideration. Use the "Metric Units" tab for the International System of Units or the "Other Units" tab to convert units into either US or metric units. Note that the calculator also computes the Ponderal Index in addition to BMI, both of which are discussed below in detail.

Cara menggunakan Kalkulator BMI DokterSehat: -Masukkan tinggi badan Anda (dalam satuan cm) -Masukkan berat badan Anda (dalam satuan kg) -Masukkan Usia Anda. Klik “Submit!” Hasil perhitungan BMI Anda akan muncul Hasil perhitungan BMI akan disesuaikan dengan tinggi badan dan berat badan yang Anda masukkan ke dalam kalkulator. Selanjutnya, Anda bisa mengetahui apakah Anda tergolong kurus, ideal, gemuk, atau obesitas.
